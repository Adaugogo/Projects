Biography

Ukaumunna Favour Ugochi is a 23-year-old Nigerian researcher, educator, and aspiring geneticist with a background in microbiology and bioinformatics. She graduated with a First-Class degree from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, and has experience in teaching, laboratory research, and administrative support. Passionate about genomics and molecular biology, she aims to contribute to health tech advancements and biomedical research. She is also an active mentor and advocate for education, youth development, and women's empowerment in STEM. Currently, she is applying for graduate programs to further her studies and research in genetics and bioinformatics.
